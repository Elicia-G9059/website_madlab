About Us

We believe that eating fruits and minerals is extremely important for your health - but it definitely doesn’t have to be boring! Food should be colourful, tasty and enjoyable, not confusing or dull. Our goal is to make healthy eating simple and fun by showing how everyday fruits and mineral-rich foods help your body feel its best. If you’re ever stuck on what to make, our recipe page is here to help and our quick quiz makes choosing a meal easy and stress-free. Healthy food and drinks should feel good, taste good and fit into real life, which is exactly what we’re all about!

Learn about some Vitamins and Minerals

Vitamins

Vitamin A

What it does: Helps you see well, keeps skin healthy, helps fight infections

Found in: Carrots, sweet potatoes, spinach, pumpkin, milk, eggs, liver.

Vitamin C

What it does: Helps heal wounds, keeps gums healthy, boosts immunity.

Found in: Oranges, strawberries, kiwi, peppers, broccoli.

Vitamin D

What it does: Helps bones and teeth stay strong.

Found in: Sunlight, fatty fish (salmon), eggs, fortified milk.

Vitamin E

What it does: Protects cells and keeps skin healthy.

Found in: Nuts, seeds, vegetable oils, spinach.

Vitamin K

What it does: Helps blood clot and supports bone health.

Found in: Leafy green vegetables like spinach and kale.

Minerals

Calcium

What it does: Builds strong bones and teeth.

Found in: Milk, cheese, yogurt, leafy greens, fortified plant milks.

Iron

What it does: Helps carry oxygen in the blood and prevents tiredness.

Found in: Red meat, beans, lentils, spinach, fortified cereals.

Potassium

What it does: Helps muscles, nerves and heart work properly.

Found in: Bananas, potatoes, oranges, beans, yogurt.

Magnesium

What it does: Helps muscles relax and supports energy production.

Found in: Nuts, seeds, whole grains, leafy greens.

Zinc

What it does: Helps immune system and wound healing.

Found in: Meat, shellfish, beans, nuts, seeds.

Iodine

What it does: Helps the thyroid control metabolism.

Found in: Iodized salt, seafood, dairy products.

Sodium

What it does: Helps control fluids and nerve signals.

Found in: Salt, processed foods (often too much).

Phosphorus

What it does: Works with calcium to build bones and teeth.

Found in: Meat, dairy, nuts, beans.

Selenium

What it does: Protects cells and supports immunity.

Found in: Brazil nuts, fish, meat, eggs.
